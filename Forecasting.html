Comparison of Forecasting Models and Topic Modelling

The project aimed to enhance decision-making in two areas. First, forecasting U.S. Personal Consumption Expenditures (PCE), crucial for economic planning, required developing robust models to ensure accurate predictions while addressing data anomalies. Second, analyzing hotel customer reviews focused on identifying key factors driving satisfaction and dissatisfaction to improve service quality and customer experience. Challenges included selecting accurate forecasting models and extracting meaningful insights from unstructured data using sentiment analysis and topic modeling. These efforts provided actionable insights for economic planning and hospitality management, showcasing data-driven problem-solving expertise.
